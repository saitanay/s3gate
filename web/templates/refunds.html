Refunds & Cancellations

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Refund Policy

BucketCheap operates on a prepaid wallet model. Wallet recharges are eligible for refund under the following conditions:

  • Unused credits: If you have not used any storage after recharge, you may request a full refund within 7 days of payment.
  • Partial refunds: If you have used the service, we will refund the unused wallet balance (minus any consumed months at ₹99/month).
  • Processing time: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Cancellation Policy

  • You may cancel your account at any time from the dashboard settings.
  • Upon cancellation, your remaining wallet balance will be refunded (minus consumed usage).
  • Your data will be retained for 7 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted.
  • Free trial users can cancel without any charges.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at bucketcheap@tanay.co.in with your registered email and reason for refund. We will process it within 2 business days.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Accounts terminated for Terms & Conditions violation
  • Refund requests made more than 30 days after payment
